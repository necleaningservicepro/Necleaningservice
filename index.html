About Me

Sandra Flórez

Sandra Flórez is an experienced cleaning professional with over a decade of industry expertise. Her commitment to excellence and attention to detail has established her as a trusted reference in the care of high-standard spaces throughout Manhattan.

With a long-standing career in New York's professional cleaning industry, Sandra is a recognized expert in the meticulous care of luxury properties. She has provided top-tier cleaning services in prestigious Manhattan hotels, exclusive residences across the city, and premium wellness spaces, including the iconic Fekkai salon at The Mark Hotel.

Her dedication to detail and excellence has built a solid reputation as a specialist in the maintenance of luxury spaces. Today, Sandra focuses entirely on bringing that same level of professionalism and excellence to every home she serves.
